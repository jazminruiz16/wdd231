Website Plan

Site Name:

Organic Shop Bolivia

Site Purpose:

Ecotierra is my organic products venture; we produce vegetables and aromatic plants in Viacha, Bolivia. The purpose of this project is to design a friendly webpage that shows our products and their benefits. And encourage people to want to know more about us.

Scenarios:

• How can I improve my nutrition with organic products?
• What organic products does the shop offer?
• Where can I find contact information in order to get the products?

Color Schema

General description:

The palette of colors is in the hexadecimal system. Colors are related to organic products, like green and earthy colors.

Background colors:

#adb993 (it is the primary color in the page), #343633 (the secondary color in the page, used in the footer and sections), #949d6a (this color is used to accent some sections, and buttons),#d0d38f (this color will be used in aside sections).

Text colors:

#000000 for heading 1 and text with background of light color, #f6ca83 for other headings and text with dark color background.

Typography

General description:

The page will have two fonts: Times New Roman and Lato sans serif

Times New Roman:

This font will be use for the modal dialogs

Lato sans serif:

This font will be use for for the rest of the page.
